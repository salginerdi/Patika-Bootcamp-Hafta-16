My Favourite Music Groups

Red Hot Chili Peppers

Red Hot Chili Peppers (RHCP), formed in 1983, is an American band that blends funk, punk and metal. The founders of the band are Anthony Kiedis, Michael Balzary, Hillel Slovak and Jack Irons.

Their first name was 'Tony Flow and the Miraculously Majestic Masters of Mayhem' and the band signed a contract with EMI record label at the end of the same year. They later changed their name to Red Hot Chili Peppers.
